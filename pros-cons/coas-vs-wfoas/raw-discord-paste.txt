**Carrot on a Stick Pros:**
• Is easier to type out the name (`carrot_on_a_stick` vs. `warped_fungus_on_a_stick`)
• Only attracts Pigs, which is unlikely to be in the Nether and also unlikely to be in the End
• Has 25 durability (May be useful)

**Warped Fungus on a Stick Pros:**
• Only attracts Striders, which is VERY unlikely to be in the Overworld and more unlikely to be in the End
• Has 100 durability (May be useful)
