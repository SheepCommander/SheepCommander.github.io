**AEC Pros:**
• Slightly Less lag
• Can be invisible even in spectator

**Armor Stand Pros:**
• Can be easier to visualise (Head rotation)
• Can be easier to debug (Visible, or at least visible in Spectator)
• Won't run out and die, unlike AECs
