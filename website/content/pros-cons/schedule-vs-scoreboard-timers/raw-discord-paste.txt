**/Schedule Pros:**
• Doesn't require setup
• Can be simpler to use
• Actually more efficient than scoreboard timers

**Cons:** (Everything /Schedule doesn't have that Scoreboard Timers do)
• Doesn't remember who ran it
• Can't label it
• Can't save how far into the countdown it was when stopped
