**Ranges**
`1` = exactly 1
`1..` = 1 or more
`..1` = 1 or less
`1..10` = 1 to 10
Examples:
> `execute if score <scoreboard_player> <objective> matches 1..`
> `@a[distance=..5]`
**Exclusive Range (Scores)**
`execute unless score <scoreboard_player> <objective> matches 1..5 ...`
